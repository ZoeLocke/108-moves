---
title: Opening of Tai Chi
weight: 1
learned: true
direction: Front
steps: [Raise arms, lower arms, turn to right, push out and down]
transition_note: Step up to hold ball on right foot
build:
    render: never
---
1. Lift arms up and sit slightly,
2. lower arms and raise up,
3. turn to the right sliding the back heel around and bring the right arm up and the left arm down,
4. push out with the right arm and down with the left arm.
